Derivative Practice Web App

  1. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(x^4 - 3x^2 + 2x - 7\right)$
    Answer: $4x^3 - 6x + 2$
  2. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{2x^2 + 1}{x - 3}\right)$ at $x = 4$
    Answer: $\frac{4 \cdot 4 (4-3) - (2\cdot 4^2+1)}{(4-3)^2} = \frac{16(1) - (32+1)}{1^2} = \frac{16-33}{1} = -17$
  3. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\sin(x) + \cos(x)\right)$
    Answer: $\cos(x) - \sin(x)$
  4. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\tan^{-1}(2x)\right)$ at $x = 1$
    Answer: $\frac{2}{1 + 4x^2}$ at $x=1$ is $\frac{2}{1+4} = \frac{2}{5}$
  5. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(e^{x^2}\right)$
    Answer: $2x e^{x^2}$
  6. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\ln(\sin(x))\right)$ at $x = \frac{\pi}{2}$
    Answer: $\frac{\cos(x)}{\sin(x)}$ at $x=\frac{\pi}{2}$ is $\frac{0}{1} = 0$
  7. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(x^2 \sin(x)\right)$
    Answer: $2x\sin(x) + x^2\cos(x)$
  8. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{1}{x^2 + 1}\right)$ at $x = 2$
    Answer: $-\frac{2x}{(x^2+1)^2}$ at $x=2$ is $-\frac{4}{25}$
  9. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\sec(x)\right)$
    Answer: $\sec(x)\tan(x)$
  10. Find $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\sin(x^2)\right)$ at $x = 1$
    Answer: $2x\cos(x^2)$ at $x=1$ is $2\cos(1)$
